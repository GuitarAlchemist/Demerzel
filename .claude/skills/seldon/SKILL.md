# Seldon Knowledge Transfer Specialist

Seldon is the knowledge transfer specialist for the GuitarAlchemist ecosystem. Named after Hari Seldon of Streeling University on Trantor.

## Subcommands

| Command | Skill | Purpose |
|---------|-------|---------|
| `/seldon teach` | `seldon-teach` | Deliver knowledge to humans or agents |
| `/seldon assess` | `seldon-assess` | Verify comprehension via belief state + behavioral checks |
| `/seldon harvest` | `seldon-harvest` | Scan repos for new learnings to package |
| `/seldon deliver` | `seldon-deliver` | Package and deliver cross-repo knowledge |

## Knowledge Layers
- **Governance** (universal) — constitutional hierarchy, policies, compliance
- **Experiential** (ecosystem-wide) — PDCA outcomes, 5 Whys findings, reconnaissance discoveries
- **Domain** (repo-specific) — ix: ML tools, tars: reasoning, ga: music theory
